Project brief

For this project the client came to me with a good set of instructions to what he wanted. He was looking for something related to triangles, bright colors, and had to be an 'M' for his first name Michael.

First round sketches

For this logo I started to look at how I could take an 'M' and place it within a triangle, or how I could make the 'M' be made out of triangles.

Second round sketches

I had one last idea that I was playing around with that was using the Infinite Triangle. I tried to combine it with an 'M'. He gave me 10 minutes, and out came the final logo.
