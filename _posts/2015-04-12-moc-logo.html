Project brief

For this project the client came to me with a good set of instructions to what he wanted, He was looking for something related to triangles, bright colours, and had to me a M for his first name Michael.

First round sketches

For this logo I started to look at how I could take a M and place it with in a triangle or how I could make it be made out of them.

Second round sketches

I had one last Idea that I was doodling around with that was using the Infinite Triangle and combining it with a M. He gave me 10 minutes, and out came the final logo.
