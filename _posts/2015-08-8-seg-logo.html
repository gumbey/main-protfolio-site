Project brief

This project was open for me to do what I thought looked well for my friends and me. It was going to be used for a live stream and Youtube group of about four to five people. This was a single day project.

Research

This project didn't have a lot of research behind it. I wanted this to be simple, clean and bold.

First round sketches

I wanted to focus on the three letters to make a word mark. I also wanted them to sit inside one solid shape.

Second round sketches

I liked how the shapes worked inside of a rectangle. But I wanted something so I took the same three sized circles holding the letters.
